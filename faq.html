Frequently Asked Questions

What is a genetic circuit?

A genetic circuit is a combination of biological parts that control gene expression in cells — like logic gates in electronics but for living systems.

Who can use BARA?

BARA is designed for students, researchers, DIY bio enthusiasts — anyone interested in designing synthetic biology systems, regardless of lab access or funding.

Is BARA open-source?

Yes! BARA’s goal is to remain accessible and open to community contributions as it evolves.

Does BARA generate real circuits?

Currently, BARA predicts behavior and suggests designs. Future versions aim to offer complete prompt-to-circuit generation.

How can I contribute?

We’re always looking for collaborators. Reach out via email if you want to help develop, test, or share ideas.
