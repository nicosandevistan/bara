Project Overview

BARA is a predictive modeling tool designed to identify the function of genetic circuits based on their design. The system uses public datasets from the iGEM Registry of Standard Biological Parts and SynBioHub to learn from existing circuit-function relationships.

Vision

To create a novel, personalized recombinase circuit design assistant that supports my synthetic biology research with validated predictions and wet-lab applications.
