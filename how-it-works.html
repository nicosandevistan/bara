How It Works

  1. Collect Data: Gather genetic circuit information from iGEM and SynBioHub repositories.
  2. Engineer Features: Translate biological parts into mathematical and structural properties.
  3. Train AI Models: Use machine learning models to learn circuit-function relationships.
  4. Predict Function: Input new designs and receive predictions of circuit behavior.

Future iterations of BARA will allow for text-based genetic circuit generation, simplifying the design process for non-experts.
